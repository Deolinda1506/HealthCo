Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting the "Book Appointment" page, selecting your preferred doctor, and choosing a date and time.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, appointments can be canceled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Go to "My Appointments" to manage your bookings.

Is my information secure on this platform?

We prioritize your privacy and security. All data is encrypted and handled in accordance with data protection laws.
